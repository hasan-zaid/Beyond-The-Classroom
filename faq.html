The key objectives of Beyond the Classroom are as follows: Develop a web-based system for managing high school students' preparations for university. Increase efficiency in managing educational resources and tools using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Enhance user account management, including students, tutors, admins, and guests, using PHP. Ensure a secure system that protects users' personal information and maintains organizational privacy. Simplify system navigation, making it easy for users to find the resources they need.

Beyond the Classroom has been in operation since 2018, serving high school students and helping them prepare for their bachelor's degree.

Beyond the Classroom distinguishes itself through its modern approach to education. We combine traditional teaching methods with a responsive web-based system that enhances student learning and streamlines daily operations.

Beyond the Classroom offers a wide range of educational resources, including interactive learning materials, practice exams, study guides, and access to expert tutors. Our goal is to provide students with comprehensive support to excel in their studies.

Beyond the Classroom prioritizes the security and privacy of user information. We have implemented robust security measures and encryption protocols to protect personal data. Our system adheres to strict privacy policies to ensure that user information remains confidential.
